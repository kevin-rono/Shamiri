Frequently Asked Questions


1. What next after filling the eligibility form?

Our team will review your responses, and evaluate your eligibility for guidance and support through the intervention. If you turn out to be eligible, we shall reach out to you via email.

2. What next after answering the intervention questions?

Our team will review your responses, and evaluate your progress throughout the intervention. We shall reach out to you via email for feedback on your progess.

3. What is Shamiri?

Shamiri is a community thay seeks to promote wellness and resilience in our youth. We administer measures of depression, anxiety, social support, and gratitude. The purpose of this intervention is to examine the prevalence of depression and anxiety, examine associations between these measures, and identify moderators of depression and anxiety.

4. Who will be helping me?

After you sign up, we will match you to an available administrator who fits your objectives, preferences, and the type of issues you are dealing with. Different counselors have different approaches and areas of focus, so it's important to find the right person who can achieve the best results for you. If you start the process and you feel your counselor isn't a good fit for you, you may request to be matched to a different counselor.

5. Is Shamiri right for me?

Shamiri may be right for you if you're looking to improve the quality of your life. Whenever there is anything that interferes with your happiness or prevents you from achieving your goals, we may be able to help.
Shamiri is not the right solution for you if any of the following is true:

  • You are a minor or you are under the care of a legal guardian.
  • You do not have a device that can connect to the Internet or you do not have a reliable Internet connection.
  • You have been diagnosed with a severe mental illness, or if you have been advised to be in psychological supervision or psychiatric care.
  • You are in an urgent crisis or an emergency situation.
  • You have thoughts of hurting yourself or others.
  • You were required to undergo therapy or counseling either by a court order or by any other authority.

6. I filled the form, how long until I hear from Shamiri?

It generally takes around 24 hours to be matched with a administrator, and on some occasions might take a little longer depending on the availability of our administrators.

7. How long can I use Shamiri?

Generally, you can use it until you finish the intervention. However, this depends on your needs and varies a lot from one person to another. Some people feel they get most of the value after just a few weeks, while others prefer to stick to the program for an extended period of time. This is completely up to you.

8. Do I have to pay to take part in the intervention?

Shamiri is a 100% free platform, you do not have to pay anything. But if you'd like to donate to our cause, you can find our contacts here.

9. How's my privacy protected?

All the information you provide to us is kept in absolute confidentiality. We won't show any of your information to any other person that is not part of our team.

10. How can I provide my services as an administrator at Shamiri?

Kindly set up a meeting with one of our administrators to talk about your eligibility to be part of the team. Thereafter, you can sign up as an administrator and get right to it!
